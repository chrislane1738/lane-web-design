## Positioning Strategy

### Undercut angle
We position between DIY builders and agencies:
- **vs. DIY builders:** "Better than Squarespace, and you don't have to do the work"
- **vs. agencies:** "Custom quality at a fraction of the price"

### Why we can undercut
1. **Near-zero hosting costs** -- Vercel free/pro vs. agencies paying $30-$100/mo per site for managed WordPress
2. **No overhead** -- Solo operator vs. agency payroll
3. **Modern stack** -- Next.js needs less maintenance than WordPress (no plugin updates, security patches, database management)
4. **AI-assisted development** -- Faster build times = lower labor cost per project

### Key messaging
- Transparent pricing (competitors hide theirs)
- One simple monthly number (hosting bundled, no surprise fees)
- Custom design included at every tier (not templates)
- Modern, fast sites (not WordPress)
